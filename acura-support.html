Acura Support

Role:

Product Designer + Front-end Developer.

OBJECTIVE:

To create a tool to support users through downloading the Acura Edge App and successfully creating an account.

PLATFORM:

React + WordPress Front-End.

User Types Included:

  1. Sale Consultants
  2. Service Consultants
  3. Managers
  4. HQ team

User Research:

The target market for the Acura Edge App is Acura employees - making it a challenge to find employees who would give us honest feedback based on their experience. Many individuals have figured out "their way" of doing things. Working with the UX Research team was imperative to make sure we phrase our questions and conduct testing in a manner that was not leading.

Challenges:

Every individual's digital literacy rate is different. This site was a proactive action on our end to provide a resource for individuals who are struggling with the onboarding process. This resource website was provided to be successful and limited how many technical support help tickets were recieved.
