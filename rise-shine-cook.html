User Types Included:

User Research:

Despite the blog retaining users, we wanted to understand how to get higher retention rates. Working with the UX research team and the strategy team we were able to create an easy one-click recipe sharing component for each individual recipe. While also maintaining a personalized approach to the website. Returning users felt connected with Ashley, and needed that personalization from the blog, despite the growth of the company.

Challenges:

The main contributor, Ashley is on the shyer side and wanted didn't want to shift the companies branding onto herself. Meaning we had to re-strategize how to create an engaging personalized brand experience without her being at the forefront. We were able to implement the data gathered from the UX Research Team on how Ashley can maintain the personalized branding through copy. You can see how this specific language was used across the website to provide a personal touch as if a friend wrote it.

In addition, this site was created to highlight and promote her upcoming cookbook. Working with the publishing agency to maintain the branding guidelines they had set out while also trying to re-brand the Rise Shine Cook Site.
