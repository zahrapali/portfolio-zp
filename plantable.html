Plantable

Role:

Product Manager.

OBJECTIVE:

Plantable approached Scratch Marketing to redesign their E-commerce website in order to create a clear user journey for all user types. And, to introduce their digital health tool.

PLATFORM:

Shopify. Custom Theme built using Ruby.

User Types Included:

  1. Returning Customers
  2. Reboot Clients
  3. 3 Day Cleanse Clients
  4. New Customers (targeted and organic)
  5. Affiliate Customer
  6. Nutritionist/Healthcare Practitioners

User Research:

The UX Research was conducted by an external company and presented to our team. We were then given the task to apply their findings in a strategic manner which also fell in line with the clients' objectives. We needed to create a visually appealing website, while also address concerns for each user type. Many of Plantable's programs are weekly or bi-weekly billed. That means the user needs to feel secure to make that purchase and commitment. Clear and consise design and language was necessary to the users needs.

Challenges:

Working under a short timeline is nothing new in the agency world. However, working to meet the timeline was imperative for the growth of Plantable. My goal was to manage the hours for the developers and designers in the most productive and efficient way, while maintaining client relations.
