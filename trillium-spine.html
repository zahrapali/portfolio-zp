Trillium Spine Center

Role:

Developer

OBJECTIVE:

Redesign a website for the Trillium Spine Center with the patient in mind. Re-creating the patient UI interface, and information resource center for ease and comfort.

PLATFORM:

React + Wordpress.

User Types Included:

  1. Administrators
  2. Patients
  3. Doctors/ Healthcare workers
  4. Family Members/Friends of Patients

User Research:

Fully understanding the user journey for the key stakeholders began with UX research. With our findings, we were able to justify the user flow. Health care infrastructures have often left out key demographics within user groups. Ensuring those groups are represented was the main focus while presenting our findings.

Challenges:

Working alongside the designers to ensure the design was both functional and intuitive for all users. Healthcare systems can often come across as stale or overwhelming. We want to create a cool elevated UI while also ensure a safe and comfortable environment for the patients to find information and learn more about their treatement or condition. Sometimes an aesthetic design isn't always what is needed to accomplish the goal. However, it's always nice when we can achieve both. We were able to justify the front-end decisions through our UX Research while building out an accessible and inclusive site.
