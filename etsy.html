Etsy Design
Awards

Role:

Developer.

OBJECTIVE:

The Etsy Design Awards are hosting by Etsy an online store which focuses on highlighting smaller, local buisnesses.
Each year, they host the Etsy Design Awards. Applicants can submit their entry based on various categories, and get the chance to win awards and cash prizes.
Who wins is decided by a hand selected Etsy Judging Panel. Last year it was Drew Barrymore..The judging portal is accessed via the Etsy Judging Panel.

PLATFORM:

React + Wordpress Front-End + PHP Back-End.

User Types Included:

  1. Store Owners
  2. Consumers
  3. Etsy Analytics Teams
  4. Judges

Two Interfaces:

1. Competition Entry:
Etsy Sellers are able to entry themselves into the competition. However, based on their country of origin we needed to create specific ruling in our code to reflect specific compeition legalities.
Using geo-tagging, VPN blockers and caching on web browsers we were able to ensure the sellers would have a tailored user-experience.
2. Judging Portal
There are three levels of judging. We needed to create three tailored expriences and make sure the back-end interface was user-friendly and intuitive for the judges to use and be able to justify and give feedback to each applicant.
