Etsy Design
Awards

Role:

Developer.

OBJECTIVE:

The Etsy Design Awards are hosting by Etsy an online store which focuses on highlighting smaller, local buisnesses.
Each year, they host the Etsy Design Awards. Applicants can submit their entry based on various categories, and get the chance to win awards and cash prices.
The judging portal is accessed by the Etsy Judging Panel.

PLATFORM:

React + Wordpress Front-End + PHP Back-End.

User Types Included:

  1. Store Owners
  2. Consumers
  3. Etsy Analytics Teams
  4. Judges

Two Interfaces:

1. Competition Entry:
Etsy Sellers are able to entry themselves into the competition. However, based on their country of origin we needed to create specific ruling in our code to reflect those specific legalities.
Using geo-tagging and caching on web browsers we were able to ensure the sellers would have a tailored user-experience.
2. Judging Portal
There are three levels of judging. We needed to create three tailored expriences and make sure the backen-end interface was user-friendly and intuitive.
