Holiday Card

Role:

Product Designer + Front-end Developer.

OBJECTIVE:

Another Covid Christmas am I right? Revamping the holiday gifts for our clients was in order. Typically, we put together a fun, funky or quirky gift for all of our clients and send it over to their offices. But working from home has limited us to only creating digital gifts. This year we thought letâ€™s put together a charity market where we auctioned off all of our leftover junk from the office as a joke. We designed and developed a website to look like an e-commerce store where all attempts to purchases resulted in a donation made to our partnered Charities in the clients' names.

PLATFORM:

React + Wordpress Front-End.

User Types Included:

  1. Clients
  2. Management Teams
  3. Partners

Challenges:

Not everybody finds humour in a fake holiday market where we try to sell our junk. To get around this going over people's heads, we wanted users to be a part of the joke from the very beginning, while still completing the user journey. Since, each "attempt in purchase" resulted in a donation to our charity partners.

To do this, we designed the site to look like a real e-commerce store. We created a photoshoot to replicate previous client shoots. And, by creating a random "Gift Generator" on the homepage.
