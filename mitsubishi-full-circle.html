Mitsubishi Full Circle

Role:

Product Manager + Developer.

OBJECTIVE:

Mitsubishi Full Circle Competition is a yearly competition held for Mitsubishi Sales and Service Representative. There are three stages of the competition. Each entry must deliver a video pitch regarding the example client. Judges for each round give points in accordance with a scorecard. Those who achieved a specific level were pushed onwards to the next stages of the competition.

PLATFORM:

Launchpad + PHP

User Types Included:

  1. Sales consultants
  2. Service consultants
  3. Management
  4. Judges
  5. Mitsubishi HQ

User Research:

Video competitions are not a new concept. However, our goal was to try to maintain the quality of the video without significant loading or speed issues. These videos can be anywhere between 10-15 mins long.

Challenges:

Judging components. We needed to create an easy and sure-fire way for the judging panellist to use the backend and watch, give scoring and provide feedback to each candidate. We wanted to be able to give the competitors transparency and make sure their efforts were not gone to waste.
