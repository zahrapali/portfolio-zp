Terry Fox School Site

Role:

Product Designer and Front-End Developer

OBJECTIVE:

Create an engaging and user-friendly website for the participants, teachers, organizers and students of the Terry Fox School Run. The main focus for this site was the students and the teachers. How can we better engage students? Creating a Kids Corner proved to be successful.

PLATFORM:

React + Wordpress front-end.

User Types Included:

  1. Students
  2. Teachers
  3. Run Organizers
  4. Parents
  5. Donors

User Research:

The UX Research was conducted by our internal UX Research team. One of our challenges was getting honest critiques of the site. Terry Fox has a long legacy and many of the supported love the organization. We needed to manage their bias through specific line of questioning and removing all things Terry Fox out of the testing groups.

Challenges:

Stakeholders. Managing the needs of the users and the various stakeholders. This project required us to push back and defend our designs in favour of the Users.
We were able to justify our design decisions through our prototypes and by presenting our user journey maps to ensure the students were represented.
