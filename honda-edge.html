Honda Edge App

Role:

Product Designer + Front-end Developer.

OBJECTIVE:

To create a tool to improve the in-person and online user experience while shopping, buying and delivering a car at the Honda Dealerships.

PLATFORM:

React + WordPress Front-End.

User Types Included:

  1. Sale Consultants
  2. Service Consultants
  3. Managers
  4. Buyers
  5. Potential Buyers
  6. HQ team

User Research:

Purchasing a car is a very personal experience. Learning how honda employees could support this process and provide a tool to enhance the experience was our objective while performing our user research. Many stakeholders mean we had to build out several user journeys while prototyping for our focus groups.
Our user research justified our onboarding process and quick tools section of our app.

Challenges:

Student ID. The Student ID was a unique sequence number provided by the Honda HR Management team. This input while creating an account was a requirement for the stakeholders. However, most employees don't know this ID by heart. We needed a way to increase the number of new accounts created , while also ensuring individuals would complete the onboarding process. We decided we needed to create an easy informational button called "Finding your Student ID". This showed an increase in successful onboards by 35%.
